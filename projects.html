Sparespace

More Affordable Storage

Sparespace is a peer-to-peer web application which connects people who are looking for storage space with people who have storage space to offer. This application was a part of my Senior Capstone project for the School of Engineering.

Crypto Chart

Monitor Your Favorite Cryptos

As a cryptocurrency enthusiast, I found it difficult using a cryptocurrency price monitor that I liked. I wanted something minimalistic but also elegant. I decided to built Crypto Chart for my own personal use to monitor previous and real time prices of some of my favorite cryptos such as Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Schedule Builder

WIN Your Class Registration

Class Registration has always been a hassle at Gonzaga. Most students still use a pen and paper to plan out their classes for the next semester. With Schedule Builder, students input the classes they are looking to take, and our application does the rest. All possible schedules are built and the student just has to browse through and see which one he/she likes the best!
